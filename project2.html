Royal Serenity - Go Beyond Your Golden Dreams - Gollapally @Balanagar

Royal Serenity, our new Residential Villa Plots Layout with Club House is located at Gollapally village and opposite to Polepally SEZ, Rajapur Mandal. The project is 2 minutes drive away from the Hyderabad Bengaluru National Highway (NH 44) and Gollapally Railway Station. The entire layout is designed as per vasthu and is ready for registration. This is an eco-friendly layout surrounded with avenue plantations and designer landscaping. TSIIC- Pharma SEZ is 10 minutes drive away from the layout. There are plenty of companies such as Aurobindo Pharma, Hetero Drugs, L&T etc., located within the SEZ. The presence of all these companies along with thousands of employees working in these companies is expected to increase the value of land in the surroundings tremendously in the coming years.
